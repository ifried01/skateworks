Skate to Survive

You are skateboarding in oncoming traffic and the only way to survive is to dodge the cars.

The Cars

The cars will be coming towards you from the right side of the screen, dodge them by tilting the phone up and down. Dodge the cars and the game will continue, hit a car and you will die!
